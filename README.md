# 🎵 Sales & Profitability Analysis – Music Industry

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data from a music company to identify the most profitable markets, genres, and artists. The objective is to extract strategic insights that support data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Identify the country generating the highest revenue
- Determine the most profitable music genre
- Identify the top revenue-generating artist
- Analyze customer revenue concentration

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- SQL
- Google Colab
- Data Visualization

## 📊 Key Findings
- USA is the leading revenue-generating country.
- Rock is the dominant genre (~35% of total revenue).
- Iron Maiden is the top-performing artist.
- Customer revenue is well distributed; the top customer represents only 2.1% of total sales, indicating low dependency risk.

## 📈 Strategic Insight
The company shows strong performance in a specific geographic market and genre segment. While customer diversification reduces commercial risk, expanding into new countries and genres could improve long-term sustainability.
